Welcome

This site is meant to serve as a guide for those who want to learn more about circuit design and electronics. Assuming you know the basics, such KVL, KCL, resistors, capacitors, and inductors, you should easily be able to learn and follow the circuit articles and projects.

Circuits

Learn the design theory behind different types of circuits that use resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, and transistors. The basic operation behind circuits such as amplifiers, power converters, oscillators, and more, are explained. Calculated examples are given along with possible applications and considerations. Other relevant circuit topics are also included. Be sure to check out different articles as you may end up learning something you didn't before!

Projects

Explore various projects that incoporate different circuit concepts. Every project includes a parts life and step-by-step instructions that anyone can follow. Simulation waveforms and bench-top measurements are included to verify the performance of the circuit. The circuits are initially tested on a bread-board, and then some are built on a perf-board or designed on a PCB. Insight is given for the process of PCB design. Each project is a great way to experience how real circuits perform.
