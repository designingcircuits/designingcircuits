Welcome

This site is meant to serve as a guide for those who want to learn more about circuit design and electronics. Assuming you know the basics, such KVL, KCL, resistors, capacitors, and inductors, you should easily be able to learn and follow the circuit articles and projects.



Circuits

 Learn how to design different types of circuits that use resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, and transistors. Circuits, such as amplifiers, power converters, oscillators, and more, are explained step-by-step teaching their basic operation. Applications and relevance for each circuit is discussed.




Projects

 Explore various projects that incoporate different circuit concepts. Every project includes a BOM and step-by-step instructions that anyone can follow. Simulation waveforms and bench-top measurements are included to verify the performance of the circuit. The circuits are initially tested on a bread-board, and then some are built on a perf-board or designed on a PCB. Insight is given for the process of PCB design. Each project is a great way to experience how real circuits perform.
