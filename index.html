Welcome

If you want to learn more about circuit design then this is the place for you! This site is meant to teach you how to design circuits. I will focus mainly on analog circuits, IC and board level, since I find analog design fascinating.

Circuits

Learn the design theory the behind different types of circuits that use resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, MOSFETs, and BJTs. The basic operation behind amplifiers, power converters, oscillators, and more, are explained. Be sure to check out various posts as you may end up learning something you didn't before!

Projects

Explore many projects that incorporate the circuit concepts that you have learned. Every project includes a parts list and step-by-step instructions, allowing you to make the project on your own. Each design aspect and choice is thoroughly explained, so you can understand the design process. Each project is a great way to get your hands dirty and start building electronics. Explore multiple projects that incorpoarte important circuit concepts. Projects are either at the IC level or board level. Each project has a thorough step-by-step process

Resources

Take a look at the different resources available to you when it comes to circuits. Posts include how to use and reviews of the most common software and equipment relevant to electronics to get you started designing. There are a good amount of links to useful third-party websites given to help your learning. These resources are just a few clicks away!
