Designing Circuits is a project dedicated to teaching the application of analog and digital circuits.

My name is Sergio Silva, and I attend the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Class of 2018, where I study Electrical Engineering. My passion lies in circuit design, mainly analog. I have taken courses in analog/digital IC design, power electronics, RF circuit design, and control systems. Here is a copy of my resume.

With this website, I hope to improve my circuit design skills as well as help others who wish to learn more about circuits. I post articles and projects on my sparetime, but it becomes harder to do so during the school semester. Each of the included projects on the site are my own works, and they make for good projects that teach you about that circuit. The articles are meant to give you the background knowledge in order to understand the projects, but once you know enough, you can also make your own project!
